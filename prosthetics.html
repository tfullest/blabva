What is a Prosthetic?

A Prosthetic device is a device that replaces a part of the body, whereas an "orthosis" or "orthotic" helps a part of the body that's already there to work better. You can see there are a lot of different kinds, here are some of our favorites that we provide: Click here to learn more about Orthotics!
